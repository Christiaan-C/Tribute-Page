Arnold Schwarzenegger

“I'll be back.”

Here's a time line of Arnold Schwarzenegger's life:

  • 30 July 1947 - Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger was born in Thal, Styria, Austria.
  • 1961 - He starts lifting weights.
  • 1965 - To fulfill the one year of service required at the time of all 18-year-old Austrian males, he joins the Austrian Army. He won the Junior Mr. Europe contest, during his army service.
  • 1966- - He won Mr. Europe at age 19. He would go on to compete in many bodybuilding contests, and win most of them. His bodybuilding victories included five Mr. Universe wins , 1 – IFBB [USA]), and seven Mr. Olympia wins.
  • 1980 - He officially retired from bodybuilding in 1980 after winning Mr. Olympia for the seventh time.
  • 1970 - Arnold started his acting career with a role in the movie, Hercules in New York. He would get roles in a few movies, but he had a very thick accent.
  • 1982 - In Conan the Barbarian he got his real breakthrough to acting stardom. He played his acting signature role in 1984 in The Terminator, and continued his stardom in movies like, Commando, Predator, and many more. He is still appearing in movies and television series now and then.
  • 2003 - Arnold Schwarzenegger became the 38th Governor of California.
  • 2011 - Since his retirement from politics, he has appeared in sequels for The Expendables, and The Terminator to name a few. He is still doing television appearances and still acting in movies

“Hasta la vista, baby.”
